Frequently asked questions

Is the app free to use?

Yes, FitTracker is free to use with a basic account. For access to advanced features, you can upgrade to our Premium or Ultimate plans.

How accurate is the AI coach?

Our AI coach leverages cutting-edge technology to monitor and analyze exercise execution with precision. Based on extensive testing, it achieves a 94% accuracy rate in detecting correct gym exercise form.

Is my data secure?

Your data security is our top priority. We use Google's secure infrastructure to store your information and do not store sensitive personal data such as passwords. Additionally, your data is never shared with third-party apps or clients.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. Simply navigate to "Settings > Help Center" and submit the account deletion request form. Upon confirmation, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems.
